Operations Report

Enterprise operations teams are focusing on faster issue resolution, better data visibility, and improved cross-functional coordination.

Key operational risks include support backlog, inconsistent reporting, delayed escalation, and limited visibility into regional performance.

Teams should monitor sales trends, ticket volume, satisfaction score, and return rate. Regions with high support tickets and low customer satisfaction should be prioritized for immediate review.

Action plans should include root-cause analysis, ownership assignment, timeline definition, and measurable success metrics.
