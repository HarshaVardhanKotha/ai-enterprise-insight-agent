Customer Support Notes

Support ticket volume increased in the South region during the latest reporting cycle. Most tickets were related to delayed responses, refund questions, and unclear escalation paths.

Customer satisfaction was lowest in regions where support ticket backlog remained unresolved for more than three business days. Escalation should occur when a customer issue remains unresolved after forty-eight hours.

Recommended support actions include improving response templates, creating clearer escalation rules, and monitoring repeat complaints by region.
